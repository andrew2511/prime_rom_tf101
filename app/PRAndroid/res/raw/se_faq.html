Using the Application

With PressReader you can download your favorite newspaper or magazine and read it on-the-go using the application. This is great for people who want to read their favorite publications on mobile devices when they travel or commute.

Before being able to download publications for offline reading, you must install PressReader on your Android device and authorize it using the instructions below as a guide.

PressReader’s advanced interface allows you to:

  • Easily navigate publications using thumbnails or tables of contents
  • Flip pages, pan and zoom in to view any page, article or photo in stunning clarity
  • View full graphics or easy-to-read text views
  • Access on-demand audio
  • Jump to next page using article hyperlinks
  • Share articles by email

Authorizing Your Device

You will need a valid PressDisplay.com account to use PressReader. This account will allow you to read your purchased/downloaded publications not only through your Android device, but on all PressReader authorized devices or computers.

To Authorize the device:

  • Tap the “Settings” tab
  • Tap “Accounts”
  • Tap “Authorize”
  • Enter your account username and password

 

Downloading a Publication to PressReader

To download a publication tap on “Online Store”.

Once you have found the title, you can tap to order it.




Once you choose the desired publication it will be downloaded to your Android device. This can sometimes take a few minutes, depending on the size of the publication.

PressReader allows you to manage publications in your personal library. Some issues will be self-managing and automatically removed as per publisher requirements, while other publications will remain until such time as you choose to delete them.

*Please Note: Downloaded publications are stored on the device's SD Card and will not be accessible if the devices is in "USB Storage Mode"

 

Subscribe to Automatic Downloads

You can subscribe to automatic downloads of publications when new issues are published.

To do this:

  • Select the title you wish to purchase through the Online Store
  • Select the date you want to purchase using the calendar
  • Make sure the “Automatically Deliver All New Issues” dialogue is checked
Now every time you open PressReader, it will download the issues you’ve requested immediately.

To Unsubscribe:

  • Tap “Settings”
  • Tap “Auto Delivery”

Tap the name of the publication who’s automatic delivery you would like to stop, then tap “Unsubscribe"

 

Setting preferences

PressReader can be customized to meet your individual needs: simply tap the Settings button.

You can also manage the auto clean up of publications by selecting the number of days you would like to keep publications in your Library. Note: some issues will be self-managing and automatically removed as per publisher requirements.

If you have any questions please contact our Customer Support Team through the Settings window.

 

My Library

In the My Library folder of the PressReader application your downloaded publications are listed chronologically or alphabetically. Flicking the up or down the list will show you the entire selection. To delete an issue, tap and hold the Title and select “Delete” from the popup menu.

 

User Interface

PressReader was developed to provide the user an intuitive and easy-to-use interface for reading their favorite publications on the go.

After selecting your newspaper you will see it in a full-page view. In Replica View you can zoom in with either a Double-Tap or by "Pinch".

A highlighted blue title is a SmartNavigation element and tapping it allows you to read the article in its entirety within the application. Tapping this switches between full-page replica viewing mode and SmartNavigation mode where links are highlighted.


In "Text View" you can tap the Menu button on the bottom of your device to access the reading menu. From here you can increase/decrease the size of the font, share the article, or have the article read to you by PressReader.

The list icon will allow you to see the contents of the issue in either Page or Contents view and allow for simple navigation of the entire paper. To share an article there must be an E-Mail account setup on the device.
